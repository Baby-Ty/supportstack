Support Panel

Let end users log tickets, request hardware, and track progress without flooding the inbox.

  • Cuts ticket noise and back-and-forth
  • Includes screenshots, system info, and ticket status
  • Triggers IT workflows like onboarding or hardware swapouts

Support Manage

Let HR, Ops, or site contacts handle user lifecycle changes without needing to go through IT.

  • Request new starters, role changes, or terminations
  • Syncs to Active Directory, Microsoft 365, and Exchange
  • Real-time status tracking and scheduling

Support Guru

Help engineers resolve more tickets without escalating.

  • Search company-specific commands and KBs
  • View ticket history and SOPs in one place
  • Analyze errors and generate responses or escalation notes

Ticket Dispatcher

Classify and enrich inbound tickets from email, screenshots, or paste-ins.

  • Predicts company, category, board, priority, and SLA
  • Suggests auto-assignment or manual triage
  • Uses domain names, email signatures, and app/vendor mentions

Support Insights

Give decision makers visibility into tickets, assets, and usage without needing a full portal.

  • Track open tickets, hardware requests, and user counts
  • Flag inactive users and aging hardware
  • Monitor licenses, feedback, and SLA trends
